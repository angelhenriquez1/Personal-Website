About me

Hi, my name is Angel. I graduated from Santa Clara University with a Bachelor of Science in Economics, emphases in Data Analytics and Mathematical Economics. I am passionate about telling stories from data and have worked on 17 projects which range from a SAT and District size comparison to an Adult Obesity and Inequality analysis. Behind all of my work is a passion for creating from the ground up and seeing a project grow from scratch, turning numbers into conclusions and ideas into solutions. In my free time I enjoy writing code, playing video games, playing sports, and writing poems.
